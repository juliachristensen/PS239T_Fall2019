But when a ruler annexes a state, which is joined like a member to his old one, he must then disarm the inhabitants of that state, except those who have helped him to annex it. However, in time, and as opportunity permits, even these must be rendered weak and harmless, and you must arrange matters so that all the arms of your enlarged state are in the hands of your own soldiers, who served under you in your old state.  Our ancestors, and those who were thought to be wise, used to say that it was necessary to hold Pistoia by means of the factions and Pisa by using fortresses.
