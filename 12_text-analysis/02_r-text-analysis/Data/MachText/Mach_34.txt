Consequently, they are slow to resort to arms, and a ruler can more easily win them over, and be sure that they will not harm him. But in republics there is greater vitality, more hatred, and a stronger desire for revenge; they do not forget, indeed cannot forget, their lost liberties. Therefore, the surest way is to destroy them or else go to live there.
