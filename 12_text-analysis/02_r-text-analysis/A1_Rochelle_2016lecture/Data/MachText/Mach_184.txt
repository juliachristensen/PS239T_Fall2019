But you must play your part, for God does not want to do everything, in order not to deprive us of our freedom and the glory that belong to us.  It is not very surprising that none of the Italians previously mentioned was able to achieve what it is hoped your illustrious family will achieve, or that in all the great changes that have occurred in Italy and all the military campaigns, it always seems as if Italian military skill and valour no longer exist.  The reason for this is that our old military practices were unsound, and there has been nobody capable of devising new ones.
